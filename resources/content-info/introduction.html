--- heading: Introduction ---

It is a web-mobile app game with an environment to earn a NFT or Non-Fungible Token called GTX, that can be converted to cash using All Pay App managed by ALL Pay Corporation, a sister company of BJB Solutions which is legally registered in Philippines.

The objective of the game is Play, Learn and Earn giving information in flora, earning with each accomplished tasks such as taking care of your plants. Growing plants and having farm produce, can be done sold in the marketplace for more income.

Players can buy characters, flora(plants), equipment(s), farmland, and other things in the marketplace.
